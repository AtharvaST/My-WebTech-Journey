TEA

Tea, a beloved beverage enjoyed worldwide, offers a comforting blend of tradition and diversity. From the briskness of black tea to the soothing qualities of herbal infusions, it's a versatile drink that brings people together and provides moments of solace and stimulation.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Filtered Water
  • Tea Leaves(Black tea,Green Tea, herbal tea)
  • Optional:Milk/Cream

Recipe

  1. Boil Water
    • Start by boiling fresh cold water. Use a kettle or a pot to heat the water. It's best to use cold, filtered water to ensure the best taste.
  2. Preheat
    • If you have a teapot, rinse it with hot water to warm it up. If you're using a teacup, simply pour some hot water into it and then discard the water. This preheating step helps maintain the tea's temperature.
  3. Add Tea Leaves or Tea Bag
    • Place the tea leaves or tea bag into your teapot or teacup. The amount of tea used can vary depending on personal preference, but a general guideline is one teaspoon of loose tea leaves per cup or one tea bag per cup.
  4. Pour Hot Water
    • Once the water has reached a rolling boil, pour it over the tea leaves or tea bag in the teapot or teacup. Make sure the water covers the tea completely.
  5. Steep the Tea:
    • Cover the teapot or teacup with a lid or saucer to trap the heat. Let the tea steep for the recommended time, which can vary depending on the type of tea you're using:
      • Black tea: 3-5 minutes
      • Green tea: 2-3 minutes
      • Herbal tea: 5-7 minutes
      • Oolong tea: 3-4 minutes
  6. Remove Tea Leaves or Tea Bag:
    • After the steeping time, remove the tea leaves or tea bag from the teapot or teacup. If you leave it in for too long, the tea can become bitter.
  7. Optional Addition:
    • If you like your tea sweetened, add sugar, honey, or any other sweetener of your choice while the tea is hot. Stir well until the sweetener dissolves.
    • For creamy tea, add a splash of milk or cream to taste. Adjust the amount to your liking.
  8. Enjoy:
    • Your tea is ready to enjoy! Sip it slowly and savor the flavors.
